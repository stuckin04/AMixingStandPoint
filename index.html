Archiving REAL music since 2018.

Preserving high quality radio source audio.

This archive focuses on recordings that were distributed for broadcast use and preserved in the highest quality possible. Instead of the fuzzy, compressed, and degraded sound most listeners associate with radio, the goal here is to present material closer to its original source with more clarity, detail, and presence intact.

What You Will Find

Live broadcasts, rare discs, unreleased material, source audio, alternate versions, and recordings that were meant to be heard with more quality than the average over the air capture ever preserved.

Archive Standard

The focus is simple: better source quality, cleaner presentation, and preservation first. Every upload aims to reflect the material as closely as possible to how it existed before the usual signal loss and broadcast chain got in the way.
