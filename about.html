About Me

I like listening to live concert recordings, alternate mixes, and demos, and I share them on my YouTube channel along with lossless download links most of the time.

When it comes to concert recordings, I mainly mean soundboard audio tracks, Westwood One broadcasts, and King Biscuit Flower Hour pre-FM recordings. So far, this site has been a 10/10.
